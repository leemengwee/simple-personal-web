My Achievement: for a sustainable future

I am thrilled to announce that I participated in the TTLX Tourism Research Poster Competition and achieved a remarkable victory by winning the Grand Prize!

The project I presented was a groundbreaking Algae Biofuel Prototype, which has the potential to accelerate the adoption of a state-wide public transportation network in Sarawak, Malaysia.

Grand Prize Winner

TTLX Tourism Research Poster Competition

Date: September 2022

Location: Place Borneo Conference hall, Sarawak

Thank you to all who supported me throughout this journey, and I look forward to continuing to contribute to sustainable and efficient transportation solutions for Sarawak and beyond.
